Blood Donation System

Online Blood Donor System is specially meant for people who need blood for emergency. Blood donor can register on the system and it will provide with a donor an Id. This system encourages the blood communication between the donor and the person who needs blood. The web application provides a way of communication and synchronization between the Blood Donor and the Blood Seeker. It also provides them with the facility of communicating with the nearby donors in emergency. The database is a vital aspect of the system.
